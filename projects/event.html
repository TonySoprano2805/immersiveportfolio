EVENT — FILM SHOWCASE

A two-day film screening event built as an immersive social and exhibition space. Alongside the screenings, the project included a room for music, socialising, and buying work, creating a more open and communal atmosphere around the films. The event was successful and profitable, bringing together presentation, environment, and live audience experience.
